Mayan: Science and Technology

Description of Project

I've decided to present my information on the Mayans in website because we are in a digital age, where no one wants to read text books anymore. A lot of our young people are busy on their phones and computers, so by presenting this information on a platform that is more welcoming and one that can relate to our newer generation, I feel this was a good choice. Also, I'm a Computer Science student, so it only felt and natural and appropriate for me to choose this platform. I was, however, contemplating on using an infographic, but I felt that things like that and flyers can sometimes be to convoluted and I didn't feel like I would be able to organize the information there appropriately.

In regards to the actual content I find that this is a good way to present the information in a linear fashion so that it's easily readable.
